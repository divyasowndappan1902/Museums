The Silent Symphony:
Works of Isamu Noguchi

Curated by Marcus Reed

An unprecedented retrospective exploring the delicate balance of space, form, and material in Noguchi's sculptural masterpieces. This exclusive exhibition brings together over 50 works from private collections globally, presented in a uniquely designed minimalist gallery space.

Featured Artists

Painter — Italy
