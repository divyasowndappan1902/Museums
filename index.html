Our Story

A Century of
Artistic Excellence

Founded in 1923, MUSE has been a beacon of cultural enlightenment for over a century, housing one of the world's most significant collections of modern and contemporary art.

From the hallowed halls of our founding collection to the boundary-pushing installations of today's most provocative artists, MUSE remains committed to fostering dialogue between art and humanity. Our curators work tirelessly to bring transformative experiences to visitors from every corner of the globe, ensuring that the power of art continues to illuminate the human condition.
